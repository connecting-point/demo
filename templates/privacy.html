Privacy Policy

Last updated: 07 Feb 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Smart Attendance (the “App”) collects and uses information.

Information We Collect

  • Camera: used to capture attendance and work completion photos.
  • Location (GPS): used to verify attendance and work location.
  • Storage: used to store captured photos temporarily before upload.
  • Account details: mobile number, company code, and password for authentication.

How We Use Information

  • To authenticate users.
  • To record attendance and work completion.
  • To display records and reports to authorized users.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only with your employer/admin for attendance and work management.

Data Retention

Data is retained as required by the organization for attendance and compliance.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your data, but no method is 100% secure.
